Indicium AI was formed in 2026 from the union of Indicium — founded in Florianópolis (Brazil) in 2017, headquartered in New York — and Mesh-AI, headquartered in London. The combined organisation is a global AI-native consultancy delivering end-to-end data and AI transformation for the world's most complex enterprises.

Our mission is to help enterprises embrace the data and AI shift with clarity, speed, and capability — bridging technology and business to turn strategy into measurable value.

/ 05 · Week 1 — what unfolds next

After Day 1, two parallel tracks open.

Once you're set up physically, two streams pick you up: the management/operations side (Tom) and the project side (your Engagement Lead).

/ Track 01 · Management & ops

Tom takes over

Your line manager Tom is your operational anchor for everything that isn't the project itself. He owns three things directly:

  • Day-off approvals
  • Allocation decisions
  • Timesheet — hour tracking via Salesforce
/ Track 02 · Project onboarding

Your Engagement Lead reaches out

The Engagement Lead for the project you've been allocated to gets in touch and runs the project-specific onboarding:

  • Project context & goals
  • Stakeholders & pod structure
  • Tooling, repos, working rhythms
/ 06 · Ways of working

Four rhythms to internalise.

/ 01

Three days in-office

In-office presence is mandatory three days a week. Plan your week around that — the rest is yours to flex.

/ 02

Tue & Thu are the busy days

The office naturally fills on Tuesdays and Thursdays. If you want face-time with leadership and your pod, anchor those two and pick a third.

/ 03

Timesheet in Salesforce

Hours are logged in Salesforce. Tom owns timesheet management — he's your point of contact for any apportionment questions.

/ 04

Day off → Tom

Day-off requests go through Tom for approval. He balances coverage against your allocation and signs off (or not).
