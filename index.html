01 · Company Context

A global Data & AI Powerhouse.

Indicium AI was formed in 2025 from the union of Indicium — the data & analytics consultancy founded in Florianópolis (Brazil) in 2017 — and Mesh-AI, the London-based AI specialist founded in 2021. The combined organisation is the AI-native consultancy enterprises turn to when they need data and AI transformation delivered into production at scale.

We are a global Data & AI Powerhouse trusted by Fortune-class enterprises to drive growth, reduce risk, and unlock cost efficiencies. Our mission: help organisations embrace the shift to AI with unmatched clarity, speed, and capability — bridging technology and business to turn strategy into measurable value.

Regional leadership runs out of two anchor offices: Matheus Dellagnelo (CEO Americas, founder of Indicium, based in New York) and Jacob Parsons (CEO Europe, founder of Mesh-AI, based in London). We're backed by Columbia Capital and Databricks Ventures; partnered at the platform layer with Anthropic, Databricks, AWS, OpenAI, and Microsoft.

Every engagement runs through IndiMesh — our proprietary AI-powered framework combining collective intelligence, proven expertise, and rigorous quality control. And we lead with our values: quality, knowledge, collaboration, transparency, and flexibility.

03 · London Office

Where we work — London.

The London office is the home of Indicium AI Europe — strategic leadership, consulting operations, and client engagements across the UK and Europe run from here.

3 days a week in-office is mandatory. Your fourth and fifth can flex — most people work from home or stay close to a client site for those.

The office fills up on Tuesdays and Thursdays. These are the natural team days for cross-pod collaboration, in-person rituals, and unplanned bump-into-leadership conversations. If you want face-time with the broader team, anchor those two and pick a third.

Lisbon is the other European office. The cadence and people are different, and there's a separate guide for that — for now this document focuses on London only.

04 · Office Practicalities

Life at 100 Liverpool St.

The texture of working from London — where to grab lunch, what's already in the kitchen, and the building amenities you'll use day-to-day. Small details, but they're what turn an office into a base.

/ FOOD

Lunch spots

  • Spitalfields Market — 5-min walk. Variety from dumplings to tacos. The default for a quick fix.
  • Broadgate Circle — immediate options like Franco Manca and Beany Green. Closer when you've got 30 minutes.
/ KITCHEN

Coffee & snacks

  • Free coffee in-office and across the common areas — no card, no pod, just go.
  • Snack area dedicated to quick bites between meetings. Restocked regularly.
/ BUILDING

Amenities

  • Facilities — bike storage, lockers, showers, and fresh towels. Cycling commute is genuinely supported.
  • Apps — use BL:Connect or Storey for building access and amenity bookings.

07 · Week 1 Tracks

After Day 1, two parallel tracks.

Once you're set up physically, two streams pick you up: the operations side (Tom) keeps the delivery hygiene tight, and the career side (your Line Manager) anchors your performance and growth.

TRACK / 01 · OPERATIONS

Tom guides the process

Operations Director — operational anchor for everything that isn't your career trajectory.
  • Day-off approvals. Requests go through Tom — he balances coverage against your allocation and signs off.
  • Allocation decisions. Where you're staffed, when you rotate, what's next on your delivery roadmap.
  • Salesforce timesheet. Hour-tracking lives there. Tom walks you through how to log against your allocation.
TRACK / 02 · CAREER

Manager guides performance

Your Line Manager — career anchor through probation and beyond.
  • Probation milestones. The first 3-6 months have explicit checkpoints. Your Line Manager owns the conversation around each one.
  • Continuous feedback in Qulture Rocks. Both directions — you receive and give. The official record lives there.
  • Regular 1:1 rituals. Recurring rhythm to surface friction early, talk growth, and align on what's next.
08 · Essential Protocols

How we work together.

Two groups of two. Presence & Rhythm shape the texture of your week; Admin & Connection shape how you stay plugged into the wider company. None of these are negotiable, but together they're light.

/ 02

Peak office days

/ Admin & Connection
/ 03

Salesforce timesheet

Hours logged weekly in Salesforce. Managed end-to-end by Tom — he's your point of contact for any apportionment questions.

/ 04

Townhalls

Our Lisbon office sits in the heart of the city at Praça Marquês de Pombal, hosted inside the Monday coworking building. Iberian operations, European client work, and a growing engineering team run from here.

3 days a week in-office is mandatory. Your fourth and fifth can flex — most people work from home or stay close to a client site for those.

The office fills up Tuesday through Thursday, with the mid-week as the peak for in-person rituals, cross-pod collaboration, and unplanned bump-into-leadership conversations. Mondays and Fridays are noticeably quieter — perfect for deep work if you do come in.

04 · Office Practicalities

Life at Marquês de Pombal.

The texture of working from the Monday building in Lisbon — food nearby, what's already inside the office, the 24h gym, and the rituals that bring everyone together. Small details, but they're what turn an office into a base.

/ FOOD

Lunch spots

  • A Padaria Portuguesa — right on the square. Portuguese classics, breakfast deals, fast turnover.
  • Cotidiano — 5-min walk on R. Mouzinho. Brunch and lunch, 4.9★ on the block.
  • Tre Bambu — 8-min walk. Vietnamese, set lunches in 30 minutes flat.
/ KITCHEN

Coffee & meals

  • In-office coffee & snacks — just help yourself, no card needed.
  • 2nd floor meal area — cutlery, plates, microwave, cups. Bring food or buy takeaway and eat there.
  • Vending machine — drinks (soda, water) and snacks for when the kitchen runs dry.
/ GYM

24h building gym

  • Open 24/7 inside the Monday building. Accessible with your office card.
  • Showers included — bring a towel, change after a session before getting back to the desk.
  • No commute, no excuse — it's literally downstairs.
/ COMMUNITY

Lisbon rituals

  • Board games night — one Thursday per month, in-office. Open to all, casual, builds the team across squads.
  • Bring an idea — more rituals will appear as the office grows. If you have one, the office is yours to shape.
